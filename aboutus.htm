About Us


The Institution

The Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT) is a self-financing private engineering college established and promoted by the Federal Bank Officers’ Association Educational Society (FBOAES). The FBOAES is an initiative of the Federal Bank Officers’ Association (FBOA), the sole representative body of all Federal Bank officers. Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT) occupies a unique position in South India’s Professional Education Sector.

Location

The Campus is at Hormis Nagar, Mookkannoor, a rustic village on the outskirts of Angamaly Town. A vast stretch of land comprising nearly 40 acres forms the campus. Mookkannoor being the birthplace of Late K. P. Hormis, the great visionary and founder of Federal Bank, holds emotional bondage with each and every member of the Federal family and its well-wishers.
